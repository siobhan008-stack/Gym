Class Descriptions

  • SPIN: Special stationary bike that simulates all the experiences of outdoor cycling indoors. This class is a great fat burner.
  • SPIN & ABS: It’s a high intensity spin followed by a blast of abs to condition and strengthen legs and core.
  • CXWORX:Exercises muscles around the core. You will also get into some hip, butt and lower back exercises.
  • AQUA: An invigorating water workout. A combination of upper and lower body exercises to tone and tighten!
  • BODY-ATTACK: A high-energy fitness class with moves that cater for total beginners to total addicts.
  • BODY COMBAT: A high-energy non-contact martial-arts inspired workout where you will burn up to 700 calories.
  • BODY BALANCE: Combines Yoga, Pilates & Tai Chi. Designed to increase flexibility, tone & relax the body & mind.
  • BODY PUMP: A power class using barbells and adjustable weights to tone and condition muscles while fat-burning.
  • CIRCUIT: Trains the entire body, focusing on endurance, power & dynamic movements.
  • BOX-FITNESS: A combination of upper and lower body work using boxing techniques. A full body cardio & resistance workout.
  • STRENGTH & TONE: Combines upper and lower body strength training exercises with a cardio workout.
  • BUMS & TUMS: Resistance class using bands to focus and tone on the lower part of the body mainly targeting core, legs and glutes.
  • SH'BAM A fun-loving, insanely addictive dance workout with no dance experience required.
  • PILATES: Physical fitness class that improves flexibility, builds strength and develops control and endurance in the body.
  • YOGALATES: Is a mix of yoga and Pilates, develops tone and strength in all major muscle groups enhances stamina, balance and flexibility.
  • LES MILS VIRTUAL TRAINING: We offer virtual classes such as Les Mills BodyPump, BodyCombat, Sh’bam, CX Wrx and BodyBalance.
