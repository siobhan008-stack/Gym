Our Dublin gym has 60 pieces of cardio equipment each piece complete with an individual TV. We have resistance machines to work every part of your body. The gym area is fully air-conditioned and kept at a pleasant 19 degrees centigrade all year round. So you can train in comfort equipment, fit balls and stretching machines.

In our cardio area we have cross trainers, treadmills, upright and recumbent exercise bikes and rowing machines. Our gym has a full range of weights machines including Latt-pulldown, leg curl, shoulder press and many more. All our machines are made by Precor, one of the world’s leading fitness equipment manufacturers. We have an extensive free weight section with a large range of plates, dumbbells, barbells, benches, squat rack, kettle bells and more. The gym’s range of weights and equipment means it’s suitable for all levels. Our helpful trainers are always on hand to help out with tips, advice or if you just need a spotter.
